Site Name

Doxa Patisserie
Doxa Patisserie is a small local bakery that specializes in cakes and cookies. The name “Doxa” means glory, and it reflects the quality and presentation we aim to achieve with every treat. It’s a meaningful and memorable name for branding.
Optional domain availability: doxapatisserie.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help people learn about Doxa Patisserie, see our product offerings, get inspired by our story, and place inquiries for custom orders. The site also helps attract new customers and grow our business by providing an attractive and user-friendly online presence.

Scenarios

  • How can I place a custom order for a birthday or wedding cake?
  • What types of cookies and cakes are available and how much do they cost?
  • Can I see pictures and reviews of previous orders before I decide?

Color Schema

The primary color scheme of Doxa Patisserie includes:

  • Burnt Orange (#CE6933): Used for backgrounds, buttons, and call-to-action areas.
  • Dark Brown (#703C23): Used for headings and accents.
  • Peach (#EDA76F): Used for hover effects and lighter accents.
  • Olive Green (#C5A56F or #BCCF9E): Used for backgrounds and product highlights.
  • Forest Green (#3E614B): Used for logos, links, or icons.

This combination gives a cozy, inviting, and artisan look that reflects the bakery's handmade and high-quality offerings.

Typography

The website uses one main fonts for now, for clarity and branding consistency:

  • Quicksand: Used for body text and navigation. This sans-serif font is clean and easy to read.
